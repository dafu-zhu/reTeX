\section{Introduction}
\label{sec:wave-intro}

At this point in our study of partial differential equations, the only physical problem we have introduced is the conduction of heat.  To broaden the scope of our discussions, we now investigate the vibrations of perfectly elastic strings and membranes.  We begin by formulating the governing equations for a vibrating string from physical principles.  The appropriate boundary conditions will be shown to be similar in a mathematical sense to those boundary conditions for the heat equations.  Examples will be solved by the method of separation of variables.
